Important Information

Terms & Disclaimer

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer

Aayuh is an informational tool, not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for personal medical concerns.

The AI suggestions are generated from clinical databases and should be used for awareness only. In an emergency, call 108 immediately.

📜 Terms of Use

  • Use Aayuh responsibly and do not rely solely on AI suggestions for medical decisions.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive personal health information beyond what is necessary.
  • Aayuh is a support tool, not a certified or registered medical service.
  • Misuse of the platform for self-diagnosis of serious conditions is discouraged.
Intended Use

Built for Awareness, Not Self-Treatment

Aayuh is designed for educational and informational purposes — helping users gain quick access to health-related insights, medicine details, and care options. It is ideal for early symptom awareness, routine medicine tracking, and finding the right specialists when you need them most. Think of Aayuh as your first step in healthcare — guiding you toward better decisions, not making them for you.
