Welcome to AAYUH

This Privacy Policy describes how AAYUH (aayuh.co.in) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our healthcare and medicine platform. By using AAYUH you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Name and email address during signup or login
  • Medical history voluntarily added by users
  • Medicine search and browsing history within the app
  • Device type, browser information, and IP address
  • Location data — only when you explicitly grant permission
  • Contact form submissions including name, email, phone, and message

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide medicine recommendations and symptom checker features
  • Improve platform accuracy, performance, and user experience
  • Store your medical history securely for your personal reference
  • Provide nearby hospital, doctor, and pharmacy suggestions
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Send password reset and account-related transactional emails

Medical Data Protection

Medical information you submit is stored in encrypted, secure databases and is used only to provide the healthcare features you requested.

We never sell, share, or disclose medical data to advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties. Your health data belongs to you and remains under your full control at all times.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login session across visits
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns via analytics
  • Personalize advertisements served by Google AdSense
  • Improve overall platform performance and responsiveness

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect some features of the platform.

AAYUH displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to serve personalised ads based on your interests and previous browsing activity on this and other websites.

Firebase Services

We use Firebase (by Google) for authentication, data storage, and analytics. Information processed by Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Firebase services we use include:

  • Firebase Authentication — secure login and registration
  • Firebase Firestore — encrypted cloud database storage
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage statistics only

Third-Party Services & Links

AAYUH integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore (Google)
  • Google Maps API — for nearby hospital and pharmacy features
  • Medicine information APIs — for drug data and dosage details

  • EmailJS — for processing contact form submissions

Our site may also link to external hospitals, pharmacies, and healthcare providers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services:
