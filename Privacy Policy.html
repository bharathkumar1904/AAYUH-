AAYUH Privacy Policy

Your health data and privacy matter to us

Effective Date: May 7, 2026

Welcome to AAYUH. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our healthcare and medicine platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address during signup/login
  • Medical history added by users
  • Medicine search history
  • Device and browser information
  • Location data (only if permission is granted)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide medicine recommendations
  • Improve symptom checker accuracy
  • Store medical history securely
  • Improve user experience and website performance
  • Provide nearby hospital suggestions

3. Data Security

AAYUH uses secure technologies and encrypted databases to protect your personal and medical information. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, remember login sessions, and analyze website traffic.

5. Third-Party Services

AAYUH may use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Maps API
  • Medicine information APIs
  • Google AdSense (for advertisements)

These services may collect limited information according to their own privacy policies.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored information
  • Request correction of data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Disable location permissions anytime

7. Children's Privacy

AAYUH does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
